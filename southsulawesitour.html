Zouxou 2025 South Sulawesi Tour Details

7-Days South Sulawesi Tour (Makassar, Maros, Malino, Bira, Toraja)

The south side of Sulawesi is full of beauty and taste! Try food like Coto Makassar, Papiong, and Pallubasa. See the beautiful Bira Beach, historical cave in Toraja, and so much more!

Itinerary (note: 5 days of tour excluding 1 day of departure)

Day 1
  • Arrival at Sultan Hasanuddin International Airport (taking morning flight), lunch at Coto Nusantara
  • Visiting Fort Rotterdam, check in to Aryaduta Hotel
  • Dinner at Pallubasa Serigala, enjoying the rest of the evening at CPI
Day 2
  • Breakfast at the hotel, then head off to Malino (2 hr trip)
  • Lunch at Malino, visiting the Takapala Waterfall & Sierra Sky View
  • Visiting Pasar Sentral Malino to buy souvenirs and snacks, head back to Makassar
  • Dinner at Konro Karebosi, head back to hotel
Day 3
  • Breakfast & check out from the hotel, head off to Bira at 11.00 (4-5 hr trip)
  • Lunch on the way to Bira, check in to Hakuna Matata Resort
  • Sunset at Apparalang Cliff/Beach, dinner at the resort
Day 4
  • Breakfast at the resort, then snorkeling at Liukang Island
  • Check out and head back to Makassar, lunch on the way
  • Visiting Leang-Leang Prehistoric Park, dinner at Sop Saudara Assauna, then head to the terminal to go to Toraja
Day 5
  • Arrive at Toraja, check in to Hotel Toraja Heritage
  • Lunch at the hotel, then visiting Burake (Jesus statue)
  • Visiting Kete Kesu Village, then dinner at the hotel
Day 6
  • Breakfast at the hotel, then visiting Londa (cave burial)
  • Visiting Lemo (stone burial), then lunch at a local restaurant
  • Visiting Bori (megalithic site), then head back to Makassar
Day 7
  • Arrived in Makassar, head off to Maros-Pangkep Karst
  • Lunch at a local restaurant, head to the airport for departure
